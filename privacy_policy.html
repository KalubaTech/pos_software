1. Overview

Dynamos POS is a desktop point of sale application designed for Windows that helps businesses manage sales, inventory, and customer transactions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your business data.

Key Points:

  • Dynamos POS operates primarily as a local, offline application
  • Your business data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit your sales data to external servers
  • No personal information is required to use the core features

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Business Data (Stored Locally)

Dynamos POS stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Product Information: Names, descriptions, prices, SKUs, categories, and inventory levels
  • Transaction Records: Sales data, payment information, receipts, and transaction history
  • Customer Information: Names, phone numbers, and purchase history (if you choose to collect this)
  • Business Settings: Store name, tax rates, printer configurations, and application preferences
  • User Data: Employee names, roles, and activity logs (if multi-user features are enabled)

2.2 Device Information

The application may access:

  • Device Storage: To save your business database and export reports
  • Printer Access: To print receipts and price tags via USB or Bluetooth
  • Bluetooth: To connect to Bluetooth thermal printers (if enabled)
  • Network Information: For optional online features like software updates

2.3 Technical Information

We may collect anonymous technical data to improve the application:

  • Application version and build number
  • Operating system version
  • Error logs and crash reports (no personal data included)
  • Performance metrics and usage statistics

Note: All sales data, customer information, and business records remain on your local device. We do not have access to your business data unless you explicitly share it with us for support purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

Your locally stored data is used to:

  • Process sales transactions and generate receipts
  • Manage inventory and track stock levels
  • Generate business reports and analytics
  • Store customer information for loyalty programs (if enabled)
  • Configure application settings and preferences
  • Print receipts, invoices, and price tags

3.2 Technical Information Usage

Anonymous technical data helps us:

  • Improve application performance and stability
  • Identify and fix bugs or errors
  • Understand feature usage to prioritize development
  • Ensure compatibility with different hardware configurations
  • Provide better customer support

3.3 Optional Online Features

If you enable optional online features:

  • Software Updates: Check for and download application updates
  • Cloud Backup: (If available) Sync data to your personal cloud storage
  • License Verification: Validate your software license (if applicable)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your business data is stored locally on your Windows device in an encrypted SQLite database located at:

  • C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Local\Dynamos_POS\

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Database Encryption: Your local database is encrypted to protect sensitive information
  • Access Control: User authentication and role-based permissions (if multi-user mode enabled)
  • Secure Connections: HTTPS encryption for any online communications
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates to address vulnerabilities
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access your data remotely

4.3 Data Backup

You are responsible for backing up your data. We recommend:

  • Regular manual backups using the export feature
  • Storing backups on external drives or secure cloud storage
  • Testing backup restoration periodically

Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You should also implement physical security for your devices and use strong passwords.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your business data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share anonymous technical data with trusted service providers who help us:

  • Process payments (for license purchases only)

These providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protecting our legal rights
  • Preventing fraud or illegal activities
  • Ensuring user safety

5.4 Business Transfers

If Kaluba Technologies is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

5.5 With Your Consent

We may share information with your explicit consent, such as:

  • Sharing diagnostics for technical support
  • Participating in beta testing programs
  • Providing testimonials or case studies

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

Since your data is stored locally, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data within the application
  • Export: Export data to CSV, Excel, or PDF formats
  • Modify: Edit or update any information
  • Delete: Remove specific records or clear all data

6.2 Privacy Settings

You can control:

  • Customer Data Collection: Choose whether to collect customer information
  • Usage Analytics: Opt out of anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash Reports: Disable automatic error reporting
  • Automatic Updates: Control when updates are installed

6.3 Data Portability

You can export your data at any time in standard formats (CSV, JSON, SQL) to migrate to another system.

6.4 Account Deletion

To completely remove all data:

  1. Uninstall Dynamos POS from Windows Settings
  2. Manually delete the data folder at C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Local\Dynamos_POS\

6.5 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6.6 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

7. Children's Privacy

Dynamos POS is a business application not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

COPPA Compliance: This application complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by not targeting or collecting information from children.
