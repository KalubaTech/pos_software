# Privacy Policy for Dynamos POS

**Last Updated: November 15, 2025**

---

## Introduction

Kaluba Technologies ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Dynamos POS application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

**By using Dynamos POS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App:

- **Business Information:** Store name, business details, contact information
- **Inventory Data:** Product information, prices, stock levels
- **Customer Information:** Customer names, phone numbers (if you choose to add them)
- **Sales Records:** Transaction history, receipts
- **User Preferences:** App settings, customization choices

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Operating system, device type, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, errors encountered, performance metrics
- **Local Storage:** The App stores data locally on your device for offline functionality

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT access your camera unless you explicitly use the barcode scanner feature
- We do NOT access your location unless required for specific features you enable
- We do NOT collect payment card information (all payments are processed through secure third-party services)
- We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **App Functionality:**
   - Providing core POS features (inventory, sales, reporting)
   - Storing your business data locally on your device
   - Syncing data across your devices (if you enable cloud sync)

2. **Service Improvement:**
   - Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
   - Identifying and fixing bugs
   - Developing new features based on user needs

3. **Communication:**
   - Sending important updates about the App
   - Responding to your support requests
   - Notifying you about subscription status

4. **Subscription Management:**
   - Processing subscription payments
   - Managing your plan and billing
   - Verifying payment status

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Primary Storage:** All your business data is stored locally on your device using encrypted SQLite databases
- **Offline Access:** The App works fully offline; internet is only needed for subscription management and cloud sync (if enabled)
- **Data Control:** You have complete control over your data and can export it at any time

### Cloud Storage (Optional)

- If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored on secure servers
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- You can disable cloud sync at any time and delete cloud data

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Access Control:** Only you can access your business data
- **Secure APIs:** We use secure connections for all network communications
- **Regular Updates:** We regularly update security protocols
