## üìù **Step-by-Step Submission Process**

1. **Add Language**
   - Click "Add languages"
   - Select "English (United States)"
   - Click "Save"

2. **Fill Description**
   - Paste the description above
   - Review and adjust if needed

3. **Upload Store Logo**
   - Click "Store logo" upload
   - Select your app icon (512x512 PNG)

4. **Add Screenshots**
   - Take 3-5 screenshots of your app
   - Upload in order of importance
   - Add captions for each

5. **Fill Support Info**
   - Support email: support@kalootech.com
   - Privacy policy URL: [YOUR URL]
   - Website: https://kalootech.com (optional)

6. **Add Release Notes**
   - Paste the release notes above
   - Or write your own

7. **Select Categories**
   - Primary: Business
   - Secondary: Productivity or Finance

8. **Set Age Rating**
   - Complete questionnaire
   - Should result in "Everyone" rating

9. **Save Draft**
   - Click "Save draft" frequently
   - Review everything before submitting

10. **Submit for Review**
    - Click "Submit for certification"
    - Wait 1-3 business days
    - Monitor Partner Center for updates

## üÜò **Common Issues & Solutions**

### **Issue: Description too long**
**Solution:** Edit down to under 10,000 characters

### **Issue: Screenshot rejected**
**Solution:** Ensure 16:9 ratio and high quality (1920x1080)

### **Issue: Logo not showing**
**Solution:** Use PNG format, 512x512 minimum, square ratio

### **Issue: Privacy policy URL invalid**
**Solution:** Must be publicly accessible HTTPS URL

**Solution:** Answer honestly; business apps = Everyone rating
